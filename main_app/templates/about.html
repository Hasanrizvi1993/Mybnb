About

Mybnb was born in 2022. It allows Hosts to list homes for guests to book. Every day, Hosts offer unique stays and one-of-a-kind activities that make it possible for guests to experience the world in a more authentic, connected way.

It also allows Hosts to list Cars for guests to book and drive in the city of thier choice.

Guests can take advantage of better discounts when they book both homes and cars for the same host in the same city.
